# Resilient Women CBO Website

A modern, professional website for Resilient Women Community Based Organization in Mukono District, Uganda.

## About

Resilient Women CBO is dedicated to empowering women, youth, elderlies, and persons with disabilities through:
- Economic empowerment and skills training
- Sustainable livelihoods development
- Environmental protection and conservation
- Renewable energy transformation
- Green and intelligent building advocacy
- Social welfare and inclusion programs

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern UI**: Premium design with smooth animations and micro-interactions
- **7 Strategic Goals**: Comprehensive program showcase (2025-2030)
- **Interactive Gallery**: Photo and video gallery with lightbox functionality
- **Contact Forms**: Volunteer application and contact forms with validation
- **Constitution**: Full organizational constitution with easy navigation

## Pages

1. **Home** - Hero section, mission/vision, impact statistics, strategic goals overview
2. **About Us** - Organization story, core values, governance structure
3. **Programs** - Detailed breakdown of all 7 strategic goals with images
4. **Gallery** - 11 photos and 4 videos showcasing community impact
5. **Get Involved** - Donation info, volunteer application, partnership opportunities, membership
6. **Contact** - Contact form, location, phone numbers, office hours
7. **Constitution** - Full organizational constitution with sticky navigation

## Technology Stack

- **HTML5** - Semantic markup
- **CSS3** - Custom properties, flexbox, grid, animations
- **Vanilla JavaScript** - Mobile navigation, form validation, lightbox, smooth scroll
- **Google Fonts** - Open Sans (body), Aleo (headings)

## Brand Colors

- Primary: `#B2BB1E` (Lime Green)
- Accent: `#32404E` (Dark Blue-Gray)
- Background: `#EBEEEE` (Light Gray)

## Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in a web browser to view the website locally.

For deployment, upload all files to your web hosting service.
