Our Vision

A resilient, empowered, and sustainable community where women, youth, elderlies, and persons with disabilities thrive socially, economically, and environmentally.

Our Mission

To enhance community well-being by empowering vulnerable groups, promoting inclusive economic opportunities, supporting sustainable development initiatives, and advancing green, intelligent, and energy-efficient practices for a healthier and more resilient Mukono District.

Our Strategic Goals (2025-2030)

Seven transformative pillars guiding our mission to create lasting change in Mukono District

Economic Empowerment

Increase economic opportunities for women, youth, and PWDs through skills development, entrepreneurship support, and access to subsidized micro-financing.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Develop and implement income-generating projects that enhance household resilience and community prosperity.

Social Welfare & Inclusion

Environmental Protection

Renewable Energy

Increase access to renewable energy solutions, training, equipment, and awareness, positioning the community toward clean and sustainable power.

Green & Intelligent Building

Support the transition to green, energy-efficient, and intelligent building technologies within the community.

Institutional Strengthening

Build a strong, transparent, and sustainable organization with enhanced governance, partnerships, and operational capacity.
