Women Empowerment in Uganda

Transforming Mukono District through sustainable economic projects and skills development.

Why We Need Resilient Women in Uganda

In Uganda, women are the pillars of the family but often face significant economic barriers. **Resilient Women CBO** is changing this narrative. By focusing on **practical skills training**, **agricultural modernization**, and **access to capital**, we are building a generation of self-reliant women in Mukono District.

Our Approach to Empowerment

Skills Training

Vocational workshops in tailoring, crafts, and renewable energy technologies.

Micro-Finance

Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLAs) to fund women-led startups.

Leadership

Mentoring women to take up leadership roles in local governance and community boards.
