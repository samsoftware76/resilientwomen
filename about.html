About Resilient Women

Building a resilient, empowered, and sustainable community in Mukono District

Our Story

Company Profile

Resilient Women Community Based Organization was established to address the socio-economic and environmental challenges faced by women, youth, elderlies, and persons with disabilities in Mukono District.

The organization emerged from the need to strengthen community resilience, enhance livelihoods, promote sustainable development, and ensure that vulnerable groups have access to opportunities that improve their quality of life.

Through our comprehensive programs spanning economic empowerment, environmental protection, renewable energy advocacy, and social welfare, we are creating lasting change in our community.
