Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives driving sustainable change across Mukono District

Strategic Goals (2025-2030)

Seven transformative pillars guiding our mission to create lasting change and build resilient communities

Goal 1

Economic Empowerment

To increase economic opportunities for women, youth, and PWDs through skills development, entrepreneurship support, and access to subsidized micro-financing.

Key Activities:
  • Vocational skills training programs
  • Entrepreneurship development workshops
  • Subsidized micro-financing schemes
  • Business mentorship and coaching
  • Market linkage support
Goal 2

Sustainable Livelihoods

To develop and implement income-generating projects that enhance household resilience and community prosperity.

Key Activities:
  • Agriculture and farming initiatives
  • Value addition and processing projects
  • Cooperative development
  • Savings and credit groups (SACCOs)
  • Income diversification programs
Goal 3

Social Welfare & Inclusion

To improve the welfare of vulnerable people‚Äîincluding elderlies and the less fortunate‚Äîthrough charity programs, care initiatives, and inclusive community projects.

Key Activities:
  • Elderly care and support programs
  • Disability inclusion initiatives
  • Emergency relief and assistance
  • Community health programs
  • Education support for vulnerable children
Goal 4

Environmental Protection

To promote environmental conservation through community education, tree planting, sustainable practices, and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Key Activities:
  • Tree planting and reforestation campaigns
  • Environmental education programs
  • Waste management initiatives
  • Climate change adaptation training
  • Sustainable agriculture practices
Goal 5

Renewable Energy Transformation

To increase access to renewable energy solutions, training, equipment, and awareness, positioning the community toward clean and sustainable power.

Key Activities:
  • Solar energy installation and training
  • Biogas technology promotion
  • Energy-efficient cookstoves distribution
  • Renewable energy awareness campaigns
  • Technical skills training in renewable energy
Goal 6

Green & Intelligent Building Advocacy

To support the transition to green, energy-efficient, and intelligent building technologies within the community.

Key Activities:
  • Green building awareness and education
  • Energy-efficient construction training
  • Sustainable building materials promotion
  • Smart building technology advocacy
  • Demonstration projects and case studies
Goal 7

Institutional Strengthening

To build a strong, transparent, and sustainable organization with enhanced governance, partnerships, and operational capacity.

Key Activities:
  • Governance and leadership training
  • Financial management capacity building
  • Strategic partnerships development
  • Monitoring and evaluation systems
  • Resource mobilization and fundraising
